Omnigrid Tech — Affiliate Hub

Retail POS + Inventory + Affiliate Link Automation — Work in Progress. Designed to integrate Temu product feeds and automate affiliate campaigns for retail merchants.

Overview

Omnigrid Tech is a lightweight, modular POS and inventory platform built for merchants who want to monetize product discovery via affiliate programs (Temu, Shopee, Lazada, Digistore24, etc.).

Core Capabilities

  • SKU-based inventory and stock tracking
  • Real-time sales reporting & analytics
  • Affiliate link automation and embedding
  • Temu product feed import (planned)
  • Receipt-level customer promotions

Why it matters

This product allows small retailers to add affiliate product promotions directly into their existing sales workflows — turning receipts, dashboards, and checkout flows into monetizable channels.
